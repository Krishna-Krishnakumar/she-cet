1. SHE Incubator Tech Talk Session

On March 18, 2024, SHE@CET organized a compelling talk session focused on STEM-based careers to showcase the multitude of opportunities in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. The event featured esteemed CET alumna, Rema Raghavan, Manager - Global eCommerce & Identity at J&J Medtech.

Through insightful anecdotes and practical advice, Ms. Raghavan empowered students to navigate their career paths, emphasizing the importance of passion, perseverance, and continuous learning. The event was held at the ECE Seminar Hall and was exclusive for girls.

Key Objectives:

  • Career Guidance: Provide valuable insights into STEM-based career opportunities.
  • Inspiration: Inspire students by showcasing the achievements of an esteemed CET alumna.
  • Empowerment: Highlight the importance of passion and continuous learning.

Number of Participants: 20

2. SHE Speak Up Session

SHE@CET introduced the empowering 'SHE Speak Up' session, creating a supportive platform for female students to express their perspectives on various topics of interest.

Conducted in peer groups, participants enhanced their communication skills and fostered confidence and self-expression. The session aimed to cultivate a culture of inclusivity, empowerment, and mutual respect within the SHE@CET community. This event was a significant stepping stone in personal and professional development.

Key Objectives:

  • Confidence Building: Foster confidence by encouraging articulation of thoughts in a supportive environment.
  • Communication Skill Enhancement: Hone skills through interactive discussions and public speaking.
  • Cultural Shift: Cultivate a culture of inclusivity and mutual respect.

Number of Participants: 30

Mode: The SHE Speak Up event was conducted in an online mode

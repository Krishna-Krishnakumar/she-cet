Overview

[cite_start]

SHE@CET launched free swimming classes exclusively for female students[cite: 33]. [cite_start]This initiative was a collaboration with the **Physical Education Department at CET** and was fully sponsored by **CETAA DC Chapter**[cite: 33]. [cite_start]The program spanned over 10 intensive days, offering a unique opportunity for participants to enhance their swimming abilities[cite: 35]. [cite_start]The success summit was inaugurated by **MLA Sri Kadakampally Surendran** at 10:00 AM on February 29th at Sobek Sports[cite: 20].

Objectives

    [cite_start]
  • **Physical Fitness Enhancement:** Offer female students the opportunity to improve their physical fitness through swimming[cite: 39].
    • [cite_start]
  • **Skill Development:** Provide participants with valuable swimming skills and techniques under the guidance of experienced instructors[cite: 40].
    • [cite_start]
  • **Sense of Accomplishment:** Foster a sense of accomplishment and self-confidence among participants by mastering a new skill[cite: 41].
    • [cite_start]
  • **Mental Well-being:** Promote mental well-being by engaging in physical activity and experiencing the therapeutic effects of swimming[cite: 42].

Budget Summary

The total expenditure for the program was **Rs. [cite_start]12,000**, entirely covering **Travel Expenses** for the 25 participants over 10 days (Rs. 24 per person per day)[cite: 49].

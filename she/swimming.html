Overview

SHE@CET launched free swimming classes exclusively for female students. This initiative was a collaboration with the Physical Education Department at CET and was fully sponsored by CETAA DC Chapterstrong>. The program spanned over 10 intensive days, offering a unique opportunity for participants to enhance their swimming abilities. The success summit was inaugurated by MLA Sri Kadakampally Surendran at 10:00 AM on February 29th at Sobek Sports.

Objectives

  • Physical Fitness Enhancement: Offer female students the opportunity to improve their physical fitness through swimming.
  • Skill Development: Provide participants with valuable swimming skills and techniques under the guidance of experienced instructors.
  • Sense of Accomplishment: Foster a sense of accomplishment and self-confidence among participants by mastering a new skill.
  • Mental Well-being: Promote mental well-being by engaging in physical activity and experiencing the therapeutic effects of swimming.
